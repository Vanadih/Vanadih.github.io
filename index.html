About Vanadih

Vanadih Bharat Foundation (formerly Lok Vikas Sansthan) is a Section 8 nonprofit organization dedicated to rural development in India. We work through grassroots partnerships to create sustainable change in rural communities.

Our focus areas include textiles, food processing, healthcare, and cultural preservation. We believe in empowering communities from within, building skills that lead to dignity and economic independence.

Our Projects

Textile Training

Empowering women with traditional and modern textile skills, creating sustainable livelihoods through handloom and stitching projects.

Food Processing

Supporting rural communities in food preservation and processing, creating value-added products from local produce.

Cultural Tourism

Preserving and promoting local culture through community-based tourism initiatives and cultural exchange programs.

Mobile Health Units

Bringing healthcare to remote villages through mobile medical units and community health worker training.
